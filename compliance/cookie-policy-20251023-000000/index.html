    Cookie Policy

    Decentralized Autonomous Forum Last Updated: October 23, 2025 Effective Date: October 23, 2025

    What Are Cookies?

    Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit our Platform. They help us provide authentication, remember your preferences, and improve your experience.

    Cookies We Use

    Strictly Necessary Cookies (No Consent Required)

    These cookies are essential for the Platform to function. You cannot opt out of these cookies.

    Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
    session_id Authenticate your login session Session (expires when browser closes) First-party
    csrf_token Prevent cross-site request forgery attacks Session First-party

    Analytics Cookies (Consent Required)

    These cookies help us understand how you use the Platform. You can opt out.

    Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
    analytics_consent Remember your analytics preference 1 year First-party
    anonymous_id Track aggregate usage statistics (anonymized) 1 year First-party

    We Do NOT Use: - ❌ Third-party tracking cookies (no Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel) - ❌ Advertising cookies - ❌ Cross-site tracking

    How to Control Cookies

    1. Cookie Consent Banner

    When you first visit, you'll see a banner: - Accept All: Allow analytics cookies - Reject Non-Essential: Only necessary cookies - Manage Preferences: Choose which cookies to allow

    2. Account Settings

    Logged-in users: Account Settings → Privacy → Cookie Preferences

    3. Browser Settings

    You can block or delete cookies through your browser: - Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Cookies - Firefox: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies - Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies - Edge: Settings → Privacy → Cookies

    Learn More: aboutcookies.org

    4. Do Not Track (DNT)

    We honor browser DNT signals. If enabled, we will not set analytics cookies.
